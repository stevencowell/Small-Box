Assessment

Your small box project will be assessed using the criteria below. Make sure you complete each task carefully and thoughtfully.

  • Safe workshop practices: Consistently follow WHS procedures, use tools correctly and maintain a clean and organised workspace.
  • Quality of construction: Measure and cut accurately, assemble joints squarely and neatly, ensure the divider fits smoothly and finish the surfaces to a high standard.
  • Completion of theory tasks: Submit quiz results and written responses through this website and participate in class activities and discussions.
  • Reflection and evaluation: Reflect on your process and product, identify successes and challenges, and suggest improvements for future projects.
