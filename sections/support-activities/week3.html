During the final week you will make your box look great. Sand the wood with the grain, starting with a coarse grit and progressing to finer grits until the surface is smooth. After sanding, remove all dust before you apply a finish.

Apply a thin coat of oil or wax evenly across all surfaces. Let it soak in, then wipe off any excess. Several thin coats will protect the wood better than one thick coat. Finally, think about how well your box works and looks. Are the joints tight? Does the divider fit? Would you do anything differently next time?
